WHO IS HE?

Whether it be singing, bassing or DJing, Aaron has experienced it all! From backyard parties, studio sessions, to the stage for thousands of bodies.

With his infectious energy darting across the stage and crowd, Aaron easily breaks the ice to fill the dance floor. After performing across countless events alongside some of Queenslands top performance professionals, Aaron knows how to read a room and make sure your event is one to remember.

Whether busting out the groove centre or playing your favourite hits, Aaron is must for your next event!
